We believe learning should feel alive

SmartCareerTeach started with a simple frustration: online courses that feel like homework. We set out to build something warmer.

Our Story

From a tiny study circle to a global classroom

In 2019, a handful of educators and engineers began hosting free weekend workshops in a borrowed co-working space. The rooms kept filling up. The questions kept getting better. And we kept noticing the same thing — people don't drop out of learning because it's hard. They drop out because it's lonely.

So we built SmartCareerTeach around the things that make learning stick: real projects, mentors who reply, and a community that celebrates every small win. Today, 25,000+ learners across 60+ countries call it home.

What We Stand For

Values we actually practice

🔥

Curiosity first

We design every lesson to spark a "wait, really?" moment. Curiosity is the engine; we just keep it fuelled.

🪴

Progress over perfection

Messy first drafts, broken code, awkward pronunciation — all welcome here. Shipping beats polishing.

🫶

Nobody learns alone

Every course comes with community, mentorship and study groups. Your wins are shared wins.

🎯

Skills, not just certificates

We measure success by what you can build and do after the course — the certificate is just the bow on top.

🌍

Learning for everyone

Scholarships, subtitles, and mobile-first lessons — we work hard to lower every barrier we can find.

✨

Delight in the details

From lesson pacing to email tone, we sweat the small stuff so learning feels like a treat, not a task.

Our Journey

Milestones along the way

2019 — The study circle

Free weekend workshops begin in a borrowed co-working space. 14 people show up to the first one.

2020 — Going online

Lockdowns push us online. We record our first 12 courses on a shoestring budget and a secondhand mic.

2022 — 10,000 learners

The community crosses 10K members. We launch live mentorship and our first scholarship program.

2024 — 1,000 courses

Course #1,000 goes live. Learners from 60+ countries complete over 2 million lessons this year.

2026 — Today

25,000+ active learners, 180+ instructors, and the same mission: make learning feel alive.
